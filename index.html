Our Fab Five European Adventure!

A group of five fabulous friends exploring some of Spain, France, and Italy
and blogging our adventures for the moms and mems!

Our 14-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Barcelona, Spain - Arrival

July 2nd, 2025

After a long travel day, including a 6 hour layover in Copenhagen, we'll arrive in Barcelona around 7pm.

Day 2: Barcelona, Spain - Sagrada Familia and Park Güell

July 3rd, 2025

Visit La Sagrada Familia and Park Güell, enjoy a market lunch, and have a late tapas dinner.

Day 3: Barcelona, Spain - Exploring the Waterfront

July 4th, 2025

Head to the waterfront, check out a bakery, have lunch on the beach, and then get ready for an evening of shopping and dinner.

Day 4: Barcelona, Spain → Nice, France - Travel Day

July 5th, 2025

Breakfast, pack, and then depart for Nice in the afternoon. Once in Nice, we'll check into our Airbnb, head to dinner, and then hit the town.

Day 5: Nice, France - Beach Day

July 6th, 2025

We'll enjoy breakfast at Co-T-Cafe, have a beach day, and then consider shopping, wine tasting, or perfume making before drinks and dinner.

Day 6: Nice, France - Boat Day

July 7th, 2025

After breakfast at EDMOND cafe, we'll attend a snorkel boat cruise, have lunch near the port, and then consider more beach time or sightseeing before drinks and dinner.

Day 7: Nice, France → Genoa, Italy - Travel Day

July 8th, 2025

Check out of the Nice Airbnb and take a train to Genoa, arriving in the afternoon to check into the new Airbnb.

Day 8: Genoa, Italy - Cooking Class

July 9th, 2025

Explore Genoa through historical sightseeing and a cooking class.

Day 9: Genoa, Italy → La Spezia, Italy - Hiking Day

July 10th, 2025

Travel by train to Cinque Terre, purchase a trekking/train card, hike from Monterosso to Vernazza, and then swim in Vernazza, Corniglia, and Manarola before dinner.

Day 10: La Spezia, Italy → Florence, Italy - Travel Day

July 11th, 2025

After breakfast, explore La Spezia, grab lunch, and then take a train to Florence, check into the Airbnb, and head out for dinner with sunset views at Piazzale Michelangelo.

Day 11: Florence, Italy - Accademia Gallery, Duomo, Giardino di Boboli

July 12th, 2025

Enjoy breakfast near the Airbnb, have lunch at Mercato Centrale, visit Accademia Gallery and Duomo Cathedral, and then explore the city with options like Giardino di Boboli/Pitti Palace and Volume bistro for aperitivo before dinner and going out.

Day 12: Florence, Italy → Milan, Italy - Travel Day

July 13th, 2025

Head to the train station in Florence early, grab breakfast, and take a train to Milan, arriving around 1 PM with plans for lunch and an evening in Piazza Gae Aulenti.

Day 13: Milan, Italy - Duomo di Milano, Fashion District, Aperitivo Boat Tour

July 14th, 2025

In Milan, have breakfast, visit Duomo di Milano and Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, explore the fashion district, have lunch at Terrazza Aperol, see Sforza Castle, and take a Navigli canal boat tour.

Day 14: Milan, Italy → New York, USA - Travel Day

July 15th, 2025

Travel day back to the US with flights from Barcelona to Copenhagen and then to New York JFK.
